Energy Broker UK

Odoo 19 Enterprise

End-to-end energy brokerage workflow for UK market: LOA, tenders, supplier responses, contracts, renewals.

Features

  • Letters of Authority (LOA) with validity and auto-expiry
  • Price Requests with meters (MPAN/MPRN validation) and target suppliers
  • Supplier Responses with computed annual cost and best-offer flag
  • Contracts linked to LOA → Request → Response
  • Printable supplier comparison report
  • Renewal reminders (90/60/30 days)
